Nici - The Platformer

Nici - The Platformer is a fast action shooting platformer game, in which nici, the player has to fight several rounds of enemys, trying their best to survive. You can unlock guns by advancing in waves! But be careful, because you're not the only one who advances in weapons. The game features a slow motion system, that once used, can slow down time to allow more precise attacks and gives you more time to react, as well as a double jump, and gun launching system, this game is designed to make sure the fun never ends! And if you ever need to do something or want to make the game a bit eaiser for yourself, there is a game save system, and a load system, in case you don't want to lose your hard earned progress.

Controls

Slow motion (Right Click & Left Shift)

Attack (Left Click)

Left (A)

Right (D)

Jump / Double Jump (W & Space)
